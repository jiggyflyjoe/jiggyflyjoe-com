keep up with jiggyflyjoe

For a wide variety of reasons (which you can read about here!), I am no longer active on any Meta platform (Facebook, Instagram, etc.), X (formerly Twitter), or Substack.

Bluesky and Mastodon are the newer and “decentralized” micro-blogging platforms that I'm currently using similar to X/Twitter or Meta's Threads. I generally cross-post to both unless one or the other is a “re-post” or there's just something wrong with media (file's too big or small to be uploaded, etc.), so feel free to follow on either or both!

My decentralized alternative to the Meta-owned Instagram is the wonderful platform Pixelfed ! Give it a look and tell me the experience doesn't look almost identical to the one you get from Instagram!
