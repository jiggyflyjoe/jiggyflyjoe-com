about jiggyflyjoe

info and quick bio

jiggyflyjoe, or just Joe if ya fancy, is a tech-obsessed, diabetes-managing, proudly “neurospicy” and introverted creative who swapped the fast pace of Indianapolis for the quieter charm (and occasionally questionable Wi-Fi) of rural Kansas. A former college student of Software Development and Visual Communications, he now embraces life as a tech enthusiast, digital tinkerer, and lifelong learner—always knee-deep in code, web projects, or the latest gaming rabbit hole.

When he's not wrestling with JavaScript or leveling up his coding chops or obsessing over the latest gaming gear, Joe can be found streaming live on his Twitch and YouTube channels, where he hosts chill, cozy sessions where good vibes, engaging gameplay, and a welcoming atmosphere take center stage. Whether he's battling bosses or chatting with viewers, his streams are all about connection, comfort, and fun. His cats—Tigger, Rufus, and Cinnamon—often make surprise cameos, ensuring he never gets too focused without a break (or a paw on the keyboard).

Joe also writes Jiggy's Journal, a quirky, heartfelt publication where he shares thoughts on tech, gaming, lifestyle, digital creativity, and personal adventures—sometimes serious, sometimes silly, but always real. Looking ahead, he's focused on leveling up his skills, launching fresh projects, and (hopefully) carving out a career that brings together his love of technology, storytelling, and online community-building.
